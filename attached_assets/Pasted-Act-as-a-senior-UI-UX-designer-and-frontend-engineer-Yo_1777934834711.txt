Act as a senior UI/UX designer and frontend engineer.

Your task is to redesign and implement a **premium light-themed agency website** for GrowitBuddy.

---

# 🎯 GOAL

* Create a clean, modern, premium light UI
* Avoid flat white design
* Improve clarity, trust, and conversions
* Maintain strong visual hierarchy
* Build a scalable design system

---

# 🧠 CORE DESIGN PRINCIPLES

* Light theme (not pure white everywhere)
* Soft contrast between sections
* Minimal but not empty
* Focus on readability and trust
* Accent colors used intentionally (not everywhere)

---

# 🎨 COLOR SYSTEM

## 🔹 BACKGROUND

* Primary Background: #FAFAFB (soft white)
* Secondary Section Background: #F1F1F5

👉 Alternate sections between these for depth

---

## 🔹 TEXT

* Primary Text: #0F0F14
* Secondary Text: #6B6B75
* Muted Text: #9CA3AF

---

## 🔹 CARDS

* Card Background: #FFFFFF
* Border: #E5E7EB
* Shadow: subtle (rgba(0,0,0,0.05))

---

---

# 🔥 ACCENT SYSTEM

## 🟣 PRIMARY (BRAND)

* Color: #7C3AED
* Hover: #A78BFA

Use for:

* Primary CTA buttons
* Links
* Important highlights

---

## 🔴 SECONDARY (ACTION / CREATOR)

* Color: #EF4444
* Hover: #F87171

Use for:

* High attention CTAs
* Creator/network sections
* Important tags

---

⚠️ RULE:

* One section = one accent
* Do not mix purple and red in same UI block

---

---

# 🎯 TYPOGRAPHY

* Font: Inter or Poppins
* H1: bold, large, high contrast
* H2: strong but slightly lighter
* Body: clean and readable

---

---

# 🎯 LAYOUT SYSTEM

## 🔹 SECTION FLOW

Alternate:

* Section 1 → #FAFAFB
* Section 2 → #F1F1F5
* Section 3 → #FAFAFB

---

## 🔹 SPACING

* Large vertical spacing (premium feel)
* Clean margins
* Avoid clutter

---

---

# 🎯 COMPONENT SYSTEM

Create reusable components:

## Buttons

* Primary: purple background, white text
* Secondary: outlined
* Hover: slight scale + color shift

---

## Cards

* White background
* Soft shadow
* Rounded corners (8–12px)
* Hover:

  * slight lift (translateY -3px)
  * stronger shadow

---

## Badges / Tags

* Small pill style
* Use accent color (low opacity background)
