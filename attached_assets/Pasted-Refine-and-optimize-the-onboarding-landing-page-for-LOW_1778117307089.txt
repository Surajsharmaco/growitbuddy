Refine and optimize the onboarding landing page for LOWER BOUNCE RATE and HIGHER COMPLETION RATE.

IMPORTANT:
The current messaging feels too performance-pressure heavy.

Lines like:
- “Top performers may get…”
- “Best editors will…”
- “May unlock opportunities…”

can psychologically create pressure and increase bounce rate because users feel:
“I probably won’t qualify.”

We want:
MORE people to complete the process.
MORE submissions.
LOWER friction.
LOWER intimidation.
HIGHER trust.

The page should feel:
- welcoming
- opportunity-driven
- professional
- simple
- low-pressure
- modern
- creator-friendly

- top performers
- best editors
- selected creators
- elite only
